Jesse McCormick:

Jesse McCormick is based in Harlem, New York. Current projects include an apartment that doubles as a performance space, a new attic in an old victorian, a courtyard house in a mango forest, and the conversion of an old car garage to a cemetary restoration studio.

In addition to practice, Jesse has taught in undergraduate and graduate level studios at The Barnard and Columbia School of Architecture, and Columbia University GSAPP in New York as well as workshops with students of the Universidad Politecnica in Madrid (ETSAM) and with Fundacion Cerezales Antonino y Cinia (FCYAC) in Leon, Spain.
