The Construction of Absence;


Exhibition, 2019.

Oslo, Norway

2019 Oslo Architecture Triennale:
Enough, Architecture of Degrowth.

With: Abundance Workshop, Khoi Nguyen


Project Text by: Jesse McCormick, Khoi Nguyen.


"In 2008, the global financial crisis made visible the forces and flaws of a society based on an infinite growth economy, as evidenced by foreclosed homes, stalled building projects, and unfinished ghost-towns still lurking the spanish landscape. The form of the city which is organized within this economic structure, is a by-product of financial transactions and its architecture which functions primarily as an abstraction of capital.

In Spain, the property market, the boom, the bubble and the crisis are all referred to eponymously as, "El Ladrillo." or "The Brick. This conflation of language into the physical brick unit, while conceptually convenient, betrays the hidden agency of materials in an economy."
