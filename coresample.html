Bi-Directional Core Sample:


Exhibition, 2017.

New York, New York, USA

Storefront for Art and Architecture:
New New York Icons.

With: Agency-Agency.


"A design of a souvenir for Brooklyn Community Board 07 commissioned by the Storefront for Art and Architecture for its souvenirs: New New York Icons exhibition.

The Souvenir is imagined as a bidirectional core sample that excavates past strata and projects physical layers of the city into the future. Conceptualized as a composite artifact, the souvenir is a physical fragment of a place that acts both as a repository of memory and a fetish object to reflect upon a dark or boyant future."
